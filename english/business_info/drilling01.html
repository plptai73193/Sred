Skillful Drilling and Core Sampling

We precisely drill boreholes and collect geological samples for resources exploration, geological survey, well and subsurface monitoring and our high quality skills are gratified with custom-ers.

A borehole is drilled into the earth for the purpose of resources explorations, geological surveys, ground water or hot spring wells, installation of subsurface monitoring instruments. Depth, diameter sizes, tilt angle of a hole are designed in accordance with the intended purpose. Though　some conditions of geology and rock mass make drilling as designed difficult, it is not easy to know detail subsurface conditions before drilling. Based on our geological survey skill and the proven achievements for a long time, we can propose drilling methods and instruments suitable for predicted subsurface conditions and our experienced skills enable successful drilling as designed. In addition, collecting geological core samples without remaining is as important as precisely drilling for resources explorations and geological survey. Though geo-logical layer and rock mass difficult to sample core specimens often appear during drilling, our experienced skill and device enable to keep high core recovery. Our precise and high quality drilling and core sampling techniques are regarded with high esteem from customers of civil services, academic organization, research institutions and private companies. Recently, we not only complement onshore drillings, but also support offshore drilling to explore mineral resources under deep sea bottoms.
