Boring construction

SRED has undertaken the Hishikari Mine's boring operations. By building up our experience in mine boring construction, we are assuming the role and responsibility of handing down boring-related techniques, which are important in mine development. Also, while introducing the latest foreign-made equipment, equipment that, in Japan, is found only at the Hishikari Mine, and while engaging in new technology development, we are working more safely and efficiently.

*Hishikari Mine

Located in northern Kagoshima Prefecture and owned by Sumitomo Metal Mining (SMM), it is Japan's largest gold mine and currently operates on a commercial scale. Since private homes are near the Hishikari Mine, environmental assessments and environmental monitoring are conducted as part of a development effort to harmonize and peacefully coexist with the surrounding environment.
