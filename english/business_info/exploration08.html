International cooperation

SRED undertakes projects related to mineral resource development mainly conducted in developing countries by the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA), and carries out jobs for technical support and human resource development. Technical experts in geology and geophysical exploration conduct surveys in collaboration with engineers of the counterpart country, and transfer advanced technology by implementation of technical training and workshops.
