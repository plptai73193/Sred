Rod rubcon

In Rodlubcon, copper highly-concentrated molybdenum disulfide and graphite excellent in heat resistance are blended in consideration of application in high temperature environment. Rodlubcon coating a screw joint part of drilling pipes and rods prevents abrasion and seizure of it effectively. In addition, Rodlubcon is used as highly- effective grease for a bolt of high temperature parts in an engine.

Usable under high temperature environment

Ability of Rodlubcon does not lower so much under high temperature environment. The lubricating effect of it is kept up to 400 degrees Celsius, it prevents seizure up to 800 degrees Celsius. Therefore, it is useful for prevention of troubles such as jamming of a screw part caused easily under high temperature environment.

Product characterization suitable for on-site environment

  • Easily applying work

    Working penetration of Rodbulcon is 329 equivalent to No.1 grade of NLGI and the softness is good as grease. You can easily apply it to screw parts.

  • No hanging in high temperature

    Because of low oil separation, grease do not hang when using in high temperature. Grease do not drop in a dropping point test.

  • No influence to applied metal parts

    It is important that sulfur compounds included in lubricant do not corrode metal parts applied with grease. In copper strip corrosion test in 24 hours under 100 degrees Celsius, our product do not change color of a copper sheet. It is suitable for application to metal parts.
