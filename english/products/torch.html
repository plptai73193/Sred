Thermal sapraying powder and torch

Our thermal spraying powder is powder consist of main metal of Nickel mixed with tungten carbide and cobalt to improve abrasion resistance and thermal stability. Hard furfacing applied this powder improve durability of friction part and contribute to reduce maintenance cost.

A specail thermal Thermal sapraying powder and torch for acetylen gas with oxigen enables the powder to be sparyed to heating target face, A metal bottel of the powder is mounted with hopper of the torch, and the powder ajusted using a opening and closing lever is sprayed .

Thermal spraying torch

A nozzure and a blowpipe are interchangable. Combination of them sutable for sprayed target should be applied. 2 sigle hole nozzures with sizes of 20 and 40, and a multi-hole nozzure with size of 40 are attached in a standard set.
